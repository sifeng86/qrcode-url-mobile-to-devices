FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to sign up or install an app?

No. Sendline works in the browser on both devices and does not require sign-up or login.

What kind of links can I send?

You can send normal http and https URLs. Sendline validates the URL before opening it on the receiver device.

Does the receiver page need to stay open?

Yes. Keep the receiver page open until the URL is sent, then the device redirects to the new page.
