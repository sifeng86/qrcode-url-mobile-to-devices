FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to sign up or install an app?

No. Sendline works in the browser on both devices and does not require sign-up or login.

Can I send a link from my phone to my computer?

Yes. Scan the QR code on your computer, open the sender page on your phone, and send the link directly to the receiving browser.

Can I send files with a QR code?

Yes, when file storage is configured. You can upload a temporary file from your phone and download it on the receiving device.

Do file transfers expire?

Yes. File transfers use a configurable lifetime, and the object is deleted after that lifetime expires.
